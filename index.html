Welcome to Plan Brilliant 🌟

We're here to help you through this career transition. Having been here ourselves, we know just how difficult it can be.

We've put together a framework based on years of business experience. We've gone through transitions ourselves and discovered that job boards just simply weren't connecting us with the right opportunities — they were too rigid and didn't have a personalized approach.

That's where we come in.

Over the next few steps, you'll have an opportunity to tell us about the things you've been working on and the skills you've developed. Our intention is to help you transition from what can feel like a powerless moment of not knowing what your next move is — to feeling powerful and thoughtful about your next career chapter.

A note before we begin: Plan Brilliant is a career planning tool, not a mental health service. We're here to help you map your next move—we're not therapists or counselors. If you're experiencing a mental health crisis, please reach out to a professional or call 988.

The Hats You've Worn 🎩

Think about your entire career - not just your last role, but all the roles you've held. You weren't just one thing—you wore many different hats across different jobs and projects.

Project manager, problem solver, team cheerleader, budget guardian, crisis handler, mentor, strategist, analyst, negotiator... the list goes on.

Focus on the hats that:
1️⃣ Brought you joy and energy
2️⃣ Created real business value and results

Write down as many as you can think of. Aim for at least 5-10. What were all the different roles you played across your career that felt meaningful and impactful?
